## 1. Motivation

In classical mechanics, a system evolves along a trajectory that minimizes the **action integral**:
\[
S = \int L(q, \dot{q}, t) \, dt

This principle defines the path of least resistance in energy-space. However, **phase-structured systems** — such as recursive agents, topological civilizations, or dynamic coherence fields — do not follow energy minima, but seek **recursive phase alignment**.

To model such systems, we introduce a new construct:

> **Phase-Coherence Lagrangian (\( \mathcal{L}_\Phi \))**
